PWA Icon Generator

Renders the app icon to PNG at the sizes iOS and Android need. Run this once after any icon design change.

What this is: phones need PNG icons in specific sizes to display your app properly on the home screen. This tool draws the icon (from the SVG embedded below) onto canvases of those sizes, then lets you download the PNG files.

How to use: click each Download button below. Move the 4 downloaded PNG files into public/ (next to icon.svg). Commit. Push. Restart server.
